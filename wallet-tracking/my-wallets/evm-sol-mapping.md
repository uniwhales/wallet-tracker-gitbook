# EVM-SOL Mapping

EVM–SOL Mapping automatically identifies the cross-chain wallets of traders you're already following. If a wallet you're tracking on EVM has bridged funds to Solana (or vice versa), Cielo detects this and links their corresponding wallet on the other chain.

_Wallets you're tracking that have activity on another chain (EVM to Solana or vice-versa)._

The table shows:

* **Tracked Wallet** — the wallet you're already following
* **Linked Wallet** — the wallet Cielo has identified as belonging to the same trader on the other chain
* **Bridge Volume** — total USD value moved between the two chains
* **Txs Count** — number of cross-chain transactions
