# PnL Leaderboard

The PnL Leaderboard ranks all wallets you're following by trading performance, making it easy to spot your best-performing traders at a glance.

_Wallets you're following ranked by PnL, volume, and trades._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (4).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Each row in the table shows:

* **PnL** — total realised profit or loss in USD
* **ROI** — return on investment as a percentage
* **Win Rate** — percentage of trades that closed in profit
* **Buy Volume** — total USD value of buy transactions
* **Sell Volume** — total USD value of sell transactions
* **Trades** — total number of transactions
* **Tokens** — number of unique tokens traded

Use the **Lists** filter to focus on a specific group, the **Chains** filter to narrow by blockchain, and the timeframe selector (**6h, 24h, 7d**, and more) to adjust the period. All columns are sortable and filterable.
